Tony Stark is a billionaire, He is a genius inventor, He is also called Iron Man, He owns Stark Industries, He is very rich and powerful, He created the Iron Man suit, He is a brave superhero, He is a member of the Avengers, He sacrificed his life to save the world, He is loved by Marvel fans,

Thor is the God of Thunder, He is the son of Odin, He carries a hammer called Mjolnir, He is a very powerful warrior, He comes from Asgard, He is a member of the Avengers, He fights to protect the universe, He has a brave and kind heart, He can control thunder and lightning, He is loved by Marvel fans,

Spider-Man is also called Peter Parker, He got superpowers from a radioactive spider, He can climb walls and swing with webs, He is very smart and brave, He fights criminals in New York, He wears a red and blue suit, He has a strong sense of responsibility, He is a member of the Avengers, He protects the innocent and weak, He is loved by Marvel fans,
